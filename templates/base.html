Add Subscriber

Add new subscribers for your email campaign, data will be stored in the DB with their status set to "active."

Unsubscribe User

Unsubscribe users to stop sending them campaign emails and set their status to "inactive."

Add a New Campaign

Create a new email campaign, store the contents of the email in the DB to reuse it whenever needed.
